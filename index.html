What We Do


Windber Kitchen serves the residents of Bedford, Cambria, and Somerset Counties, along with the elementary and middle school students of the Windber Area School District. Our communal, home cooked meals provided to anyone who walks through the door grant us the opportunity to interact in a social, positive and friendly environment!

Get Involved

There are plenty of ways to volunteer with Windber Kitchen and volunteers are always needed to help with meals, WACKPacks and fundraising. If you are interested in helping, please contact us, during our business hours, Monday, Tuesday, and Thursday from 11:00am-4:00pm.

Ways to Give

Windber Kitchen accepts donations of non-perishable foods items for our meals and non-perishable, single serve food items for the WACKPacks program. Donors help create happy, healthy and more stable lives. However you choose to offer support, you make a difference.
